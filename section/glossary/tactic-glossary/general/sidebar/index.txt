Sidebar
-------

Views provide users with what they need to see and how they need to see
it. The are collections of different Search Type properties which are
relevant to a team members role in Tactic. Views are mainly accessed
through links in the sidebar either as *Project Views* which are
configured for the whole production (but ca be controlled with access
rules) and *My Views* which are views a uses saves as their own personal
views or "bookmarks". Views can also contain a saved search which,
provides a view as a live report.
